About Valbona

Who I Am

A traveler, photographer & storyteller

I'm Valbona — a naturopathic doctor turned full-time travel storyteller, based in Chicago. Travel has always been my second language; I've been speaking it fluently since my first solo trip through Europe in 2014.

I believe the best travel experiences come from genuine connections with locals and immersing yourself in their culture — not ticking off tourist checkboxes. My goal is to show you a world that's richer, more welcoming, and more surprising than any guidebook lets on.

Empowering women to explore confidently and safely on their own terms has become the heart of everything I do. Breaking stereotypes. Discovering hidden hospitality. Coming home changed.

What I Stand For

My Travel Philosophy

01
Authentic Experiences

Real travel means going beyond the tourist trail — eating where locals eat, staying in family guesthouses, and letting serendipity be your guide.

02
Cultural Respect

Every destination deserves a traveler who listens first, learns the customs, and leaves places better than they found them.

03
Female Empowerment

Solo female travel is one of the most liberating acts of self-discovery. I'm here to make it more accessible, safer, and more joyful for every woman.

The Journey

How It All Began

2014
First Solo Adventure

What began as a solo trip to Europe turned into a life-changing journey that ignited a deep passion for authentic travel and reshaped how I saw the world.

2016
Started Travel Consulting

Having visited over 15 countries, I began helping friends plan adventures. Casual advice grew into a full consulting service — and people kept coming back.

2019
Launched Free Spirit VB

I launched this platform to share my travel stories, photography, and lessons from the road. A personal blog grew into a community of thousands.

2022
Expanded into Digital Products

After countless requests, I created Lightroom presets and travel planning templates — every product comes from personal experience and years on the road.

2024
Group Trips Coming Soon

From ancient Silk Road cities to Patagonian wilderness — carefully crafted group adventures for the modern explorer. Coming Q2 2024.
